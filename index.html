    How does Earthly assess projects for quality and longevity?

    Nature-based projects have many strengths and complexities that require a holistic assessment. In a recent blog article our Head of Research, Olivia Crowe, dives into how we evaluate and score projects for their potential to deliver on biodiversity and social impact goals as well as carbon.


    Earthly helps companies invest in nature through high quality nature-based solutions that remove carbon, restore biodiversity and improve livelihoods. Through our innovative assessment and visualisation tools, businesses demonstrate the value of their investments in nature.

    Why choose Earthly?

    High quality, nature-based We work exclusively with projects that restore nature as well as removing carbon, and create multiple co-benefits for society and wildlife.

    Science-backed All Earthly projects and bundles go through our multi-layer assessment framework – approved by an Independent Scientific Board of Advisors and verified by satellite.

    Climate Positive We don’t just balance your impact, we help you go beyond to make it positive – by restoring nature, and removing 110%+ of the carbon you create.

    Earthly is proud to be part of accelerators and networks run by some of the planet’s most exciting, future-focused organisations. And we’re delighted to be in the member consultation group of the Taskforce on Scaling Voluntary Carbon Markets and part of the Science Based Targets Network corporate engagement programme
